About

Hey there - I’m Vitthal 👋

I call my work “Software Engineering” because it sounds serious (and I’ve got the hoodie to prove it). My playground is Big Data with Hadoop, Cloud, Scala, and whatever else strikes my fancy that day.

  • 🧠 I’m a Staff Data Engineer secretly moonlighting as a Software Architect.
  • 🌍 I’ve wrestled pipelines from AWS to GCP and even whispered sweet nothings to on‑prem clusters.
  • 🔧 My sidequests include open‑source build‑tools, CI/CD libraries, and a framework I like to call “The DQ One.” (DQ = Data Quality, not Dairy Queen—though I love both.)

I’m passionate about:

  1. Open Source — I lecture apps about Linux; so far, they remain unimpressed.
  2. Teaching & Speaking — Like when I told a room full of devs that their commits smell funny. (They listened—allegedly.)
  3. Adopting Meme‑Technology—but only after it’s proven not to be a passing fad.

I despise “Meme Technology”…right up until I find myself knee-deep in it—then I pretend it was always part of my master plan.
